don't ask why it just came in my head

> _Quandary_
>
> So you have a fair coin that you found on the ground,<br />
> or at least that's what everyone says.<br />
> But on each of N times that you've tossed it around,<br />
> you see every flip has been heads.
>
> For which value of N should you start to suspect<br />
> that the coin isn't actually fair?<br />
> For which values of N can you firmly declare<br />
> that the tails side is not even there?
