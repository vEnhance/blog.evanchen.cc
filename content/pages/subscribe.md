If you like this blog so much that you want to know as soon there's a new post,
here is how you can get notified automatically.

## Mailing list

I maintain a mailing list at <https://list.evanchen.cc>
that gets an email for every post that I make.
(If you want to understand what this mailing list does,
you can read the [source code for the server][list-github].)

[list-github]: https://github.com/vEnhance/list.evanchen.cc/

## Atom feed

This blog has a good old-fashioned [Atom feed][atom].
If you use a feed reader, this is probably the option you want.

## The `/latest.json` endpoint

The website exposes an endpoint
[`https://blog.evanchen.cc/latest.json`](/latest.json)
which gives you the fields
`categry`, `date`, `description`, `slug`, `tags`, `title`, `url`
for the most recently published post.
(All the field names should be self-explanatory.)
This will be a lot smaller than the Atom feed.

## LinkedIn

New posts are automatically mirrored to my [LinkedIn profile][linkedin],
because for some reason I made one when I was a kid.
Honestly though I actually never check LinkedIn…
so at this point the LinkedIn in just a mirror of my blog.

## Facebook

New posts are automatically mirrored to my
[Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/evanchenmath),
as are my YouTube videos.
I also never check this, but if you still read a Facebook feed this is one option.
