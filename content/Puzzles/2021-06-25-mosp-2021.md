not [the mainstream buzzword stuff](https://www.google.com/search?q=math+problem+solving&tbm=isch)).
I've had a lot of fun talking with them over the last few months about problem-solving in general.

My impression is they're looking for people who have finished a bachelor's
degree in some quantitative field and ideally have experience with any subset of
{math/CS/etc competition/circle/camp, education, gaming, entrepreneurship, non-profit operations}.
They're based in SoCal but open to remote + quarterly travel.

If you're interested in this and I know who you are, send me an email and I'll connect you.
